Welcome to My Portfolio

Composition, coding, interaction design, and performance

These 4 concentrations make up the complete nature of my portfolio, and comprise the tools that I have learned to utilize through my studies. My ultimate goal is to work in a collaborative environment that fosters creativity combined with the expectation of excellent production. I have gained a variety of techniques of media presentation that include harnessing control signals from external hardware such as Arduino boards and blob tracking, sound art installation techniques, fixed media utilizing several pieces of software to hone the sound and visuals, and the ability to quickly learn new methods of creation.

In this website you will find all major projects that I have completed over the last 4 years at Tulane University, along with my résumé and contact information. Feel free to call or email with any questions or thoughts.
